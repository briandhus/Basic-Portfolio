About Brian


Brian Huston is a drummer, singer and lover of life. He has been crushing the stage since the mid '90s, and has toured the country extensively. Music is in his blood and blood is in his veins.


Recently, Brian has discovered a passion for coding. He finds the work to be challenging and rewarding. With basic knowledge of html, css, and JavaScript, he is slowly learning to bring his code to life. In a manner of months, through hardwork and determination, Brian will master all that is programming.
